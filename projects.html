Liquid Sloshing Control
in Road Tanker Trucks
Multiphase CFD simulation of dangerous liquid sloshing dynamics in partially filled road tankers under emergency braking. Used ANSYS Fluent with the VOF (Volume of Fluid) model and a custom C-language UDF for velocity boundary conditions. Compared three baffle configurations — no baffles, 3 baffles, and 5 baffles — analysing peak pressure forces, free surface behaviour, and structural wall loading.
  • 5-baffle configuration reduced peak dynamic wall pressure by ~52% vs. unobstructed tank
  • VOF free-surface visualisation confirmed coherent wave-breaking by baffles
  • Mesh independence validated across 3 mesh densities (<2% variation)
  • UDF applied sinusoidal 0.8g deceleration profile over 1.5s braking event
